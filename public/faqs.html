Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

1. How do I download a movie?

Navigate to the home page and select or search for the movie of your choice.

On the movie details page, select your preferred download option.

ADM (Advanced Download Manager) is recommended for the best performance.

If ADM is selected, the download will begin after pressing "Start".

If the browser is selected, it will open your default browser and either download or play the movie.

2. How do I find my downloaded movies?

If using ADM, the movie will appear in the ADM folder inside your phone’s Downloads directory.

For torrent downloads via ADM, the movie may be saved inside a folder.

If downloaded through a browser, check your browser’s default download location or Downloads folder.

3. Why does this app have so many ads?

Ads help keep MovieMax free for all users.

We aim to maintain a balance between user experience and generating revenue to support app development and maintenance.

4. Is this app legal?

The legality of MovieMax depends on your country’s laws regarding movie downloads.

Please ensure that you comply with local regulations before downloading any content.
