About WUSTREAM

What Is WUSTREAM?

WUSTREAM is a fast, modern, and privacy-friendly platform built to help users discover movies and TV shows more easily. We gather publicly available metadata, ratings, posters, and descriptions from trusted third-party APIs such as The Movie Database (TMDB) to make navigation simple and enjoyable.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide a clean, user-focused interface for browsing movie and series information without the clutter, pop-ups, tracking, or ads commonly found on other websites.

What WUSTREAM Does

  • ✔ Helps users discover trending, popular, and upcoming titles
  • ✔ Provides genre-based and year-based filtering
  • ✔ Displays detailed information about movies and shows
  • ✔ Allows users to search quickly using instant results

What WUSTREAM Does Not Do

WUSTREAM does not host or store any video files. We do not upload movies or TV shows to our servers, nor do we claim ownership of any copyrighted material. All content belongs to its respective owners.

Data Sources

WUSTREAM uses the TMDB API to fetch metadata such as posters, release dates, ratings, and descriptions. This product uses the TMDB API but is not endorsed or certified by TMDB.
